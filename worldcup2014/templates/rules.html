Rules

Points

  • Points per match:
    • Winner - the winner of the match or tie - 1 pts (group stage) / 2 pts (playoff stage)
    • Striker - one of the match strikers or no one - 2 pts (group stage) / 4 pts (playoff stage)
    • Score - match final score - 2 pts (group stage) / 4 pts (playoff stage)
  • Extra points:
    • Winner: 5 pts (the world cup winner)
    • Striker: 5 pts (the world cup best striker)
  • Tiebreakers:
    • 1st: total points, 2nd: score points, 3rd: striker points, 4th: winner points

Others

  • Vote closing time:
    • Voting for each match is allowed until the match start time
    • Voting for extra points is allowed until the last day of the group phase
  • Playoff stage rules:
    • Winner: betting on "tie" is allowed and means the match goes to penalties
    • Striker: if the match goes to penalties, the penalties' strikers are not considered
    • Score: if the match goes to penalties, the final score is the score before penalties
